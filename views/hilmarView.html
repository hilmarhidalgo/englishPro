Hi guys, my name is Eduardo

This website shares a bit about who I am. It begins with my birth, highlights some important moments in my life, explores my interests and hobbies, and also presents the plans I have for the future.

Childhood Stages

0-6 Years

As far as I can remember, when I was very young, I was quite different from other children. I was very serious and had low energy. I didn’t cry much—not even on my first day of kindergarten. In photos from that time, I always looked very serious.

Childhood Stages

6-12 Years

As I grew a little older, I became more playful. I started running around more and smiling more often. One of my favorite things was visiting my grandparents' farm, where I loved playing with my cousins and spending time with the puppies

Events

a. An important moment in my life was when I was given a little dog named Rocky. He was my first pet, and I got him when I was still very young. I loved him deeply, and he quickly became a special part of my life.
b. Another important event in my life was when I moved to live in Cancún. Leaving behind all my friends and family was very difficult for me at such a young age. It was a big change that taught me a lot about adapting to new situations and starting over in a different place.

Future

Well, the things I want for my future are quite simple: to have stability, a home of my own, and a good job that allows me to work from home. Basically, I just want a good and peaceful life.

Hobbies

My hobbies in this time are listen music, draw, play basketball and cook
